Typography

  • Sans Serif
    • Usage: Primarily used for headings.
    • Characteristics: Sans Serif fonts are known for their clean and modern look, lacking the small projecting features called "serifs" at the end of strokes. They are often used to convey simplicity and modernity.
  • Roboto
    • Usage: Used for body text.
    • Characteristics: Roboto is a sans-serif offering from Google, known for its readability and geometric yet friendly appearance. It's versatile and pairs well with a wide range of designs.
