WDD 230 - Week 2

Design Principles

PARC: Proximity

Apple Inc .
apple.com
Grouping related elements visually to establish relationships and improve information hierarchy.

Apple's website exemplifies proximity by clearly grouping product categories (iPhone, iPad, Mac etc.) using distinct sections with ample space between them. This visual separation helps users quickly identify and access the information they seek.

PARC: Alignment

Airbnb
airbnb.com
Aligning elements to create a sense of order, visual connection, and balance.

Airbnb's search results page exemplifies alignment by displaying listings with perfect horizontal and vertical alignment. Consistent image sizes, spacing, and text formatting create a clean and organized look, making it easy for users to compare options.

PARC: Repetition

Netflix
netflix.com
Repeating visual elements like colors, fonts, and design patterns to establish brand identity and create visual unity.

Netflix's website exemplifies repetition through its consistent use of red color, logo placement in the top left corner, and familiar navigation button arrangement across different sections. This repetition reinforces brand identity and creates a sense of familiarity for users.
